Roulette — Betting Options & Payouts

Single (Straight Up) — bet on one number. Payout: 35:1

Color — bet on red or black. Payout: 1:1

Even / Odd — bet on even or odd numbers. Payout: 1:1

Low / High — bet on 1–18 (Low) or 19–36 (High). Payout: 1:1

Dozens — bet on 1–12, 13–24, or 25–36. Payout: 2:1

Split — bet on two adjacent numbers. Payout: 17:1

Street — bet on three numbers in a row. Payout: 11:1

Corner (Square) — bet on four numbers meeting at one corner. Payout: 8:1

Six Line — bet on six numbers (two adjacent rows). Payout: 5:1
